What you can do

Everything a city planner needs

01
Live road networks
Fetch real road data from OpenStreetMap for any Nairobi neighbourhood — CBD, Westlands, Kibera, and more.
02
Route optimisation
Compare Dijkstra and A* algorithms side by side. Optimise by shortest distance or fastest travel time.
03
Traffic prediction
See predicted congestion by hour and day of week. Roads are colour-coded green, amber, or red.
04
Vehicle simulation
Animate dozens of vehicles on the live network to visualise traffic flow patterns in real time.
05
Edit the network
Add or remove road segments, change speed limits and lane counts, then instantly see the impact.
06
Compare scenarios
Save multiple versions of a network and compare edge counts and average congestion head to head.

Workflow

How it works

1

Pick a neighbourhood or draw a custom area

Choose a preset area like Nairobi CBD or Westlands, or drag a bounding box over any location on the map.

2

Fetch the live road network

Click Fetch and UrbanFlow downloads the real road graph from OpenStreetMap, including speed limits and road types.

3

Plan routes and predict congestion

Click two points to set an origin and destination, choose your algorithm, and compute the optimal path.

4

Simulate, edit, and compare

Run a live vehicle simulation, edit the road network, save your scenario, and compare it against alternatives.
